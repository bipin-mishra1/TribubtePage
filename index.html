Jawahar lal nehru

First prime minister independent India

Nehru with Gandhiji

Jawaharlal Nehru as the first Prime Minister of independent India laid the foundations of modern India. A man with modern scientific understanding put India onto the path of modernization. His contribution to building a strong India with democratic ethos cannot be denied by anyone.
Nehru carried the burden of a newly independent country on his own shoulders and worked towards building a new India. He will always be remembered for his leadership and farsightedness by generations to come.
